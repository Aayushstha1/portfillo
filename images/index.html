Itahari Namuna College

Itahari Namuna College (INC) was established in 2070 B.S. in the heart of Itahari Sub-metropolitan City by the expert team of academicians in the town with an aim to be the leading educational institution in its vicinity. The college has run different streams in Bachelor's level under an umbrella of beautiful, cool and cozy environment in its premises with high-tech infrastructural setup. At present, the college is offering graduate programs on BASW, BCA & BHM, affiliated to Tribhuwan University. The college has earned its excellence through its well qualified teaching faculties, innovative teaching pedagogy, transparent and scientific evaluation methods with modern and constantly updated new and professional streams from its inception.
